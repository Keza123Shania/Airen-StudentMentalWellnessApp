# Airen 🌿

**Airen** is a student‑focused mental wellness & peer support app, uniquely designed to help you track your mood, journal your thoughts, and connect anonymously with your peers—all with beautiful animations, offline storage, and an ergonomically designed interface.

### 🚀 Features

- **Mood Tracking & Journaling**  
  Quickly log how you feel with expressive emojis, give each entry a title, and write free‑form reflections.  
- **Anonymous Peer Chat**  
  Create or join support rooms to chat in real‑time with fellow students—no usernames required.  
- **Guided Meditation**  
  Browse a library of breathing, mindfulness, and body‑scan exercises loaded from local JSON.  
- **Quote of the Day**  
  Starts your day with inspiration fetched live from [Quotable](https://quotable.io/).  
- **Emergency Contacts**  
  One‑tap access to crisis hotlines, campus counseling, or local emergency services.  
- **Dynamic Theming**  
  Light, Dark, or System mode. Your choice is remembered automatically.  
- **Offline‑First**  
  All your entries, chats, and theme settings are stored locally via SharedPreferences—no internet needed once you’ve downloaded it.  
- **Riverpod + GoRouter + Flutter Animate**  
  Architected with best‑in‑class state management, navigation, and smooth page/list animations.
